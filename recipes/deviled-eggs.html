Devilled Eggs

Description

Delicious deviled eggs with a well rounded flavor. Hits of spicy, sour, and sweet all come together in a perfect bomb of flavor.

These deviled eggs are my wife's specialty. She makes these for most family gatherings, and we've had family run to get the last one more than once. Give these a try and see how great they are.

Ingredients

  • 1 dozen large eggs
  • mayonaise
  • mustard
  • dill relish
  • sweet relish
  • Wickles Wicked Pickles
  • Lawry's Seasoned Salt
  • Tony Chachere's Creole Seasoning
  • Paprika

Directions

  1. Boil eggs for 10 minutes
  2. Put eggs in cold water for 5 minutes
  3. Peel eggs (Try cracking the shell all around with a spoon and peeling under running water)
  4. Cut eggs length wise
  5. Remove egg yolks and add to a bowl
  6. Set aside egg whites for later
  7. Mix in mayo and mustard (aim for about 1/2 egg yolk, 1/4 mayo, 1/4 mustard)
  8. Add a heaping spoon of both relishes
  9. Add the enough juice from the Wickles Pickles to hit a smooth but not runny consistency
  10. Season with your heart (just taste test as you go)
  11. Fill left over egg whites with the mixture
  12. Sprinkle your devilled eggs with paprika and you're done
