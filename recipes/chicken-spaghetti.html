Chicken Spaghetti

Description

This recipe is a delicious take on chicken spaghetti with just a little kick. It's simple to make, and easy to do your own thing with once you have it down.

This recipe came from my wife's side of the family, and she has added her own touches over time. I hope you like it as much as we have.

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 1 box spaghetti noodles
  • 2 cans cream of mushroom
  • 2 cans cream of chicken
  • 2 cans rotel
  • 1 large bag colby-jack cheese
  • 1 bundle green onions
  • Lawry's Seasoned Salt
  • Tony Chachere's Creole Seasoning
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

  1. Boil chicken breasts till cooked through
  2. Shred chicken breasts
  3. Mix shredded chicken with both cans of cream of mushroom, cream of chicken, and rotel
  4. Season mixture with your heart (Add a good bit of all seasonings, but be careful with the salt)
  5. Boil spaghetti noodles
  6. Add noodles and chicken mixture together in a baking dish
  7. Top with colby-jack cheese
  8. Bake at 400° for 30 min
  9. Chop green onion and use to top dish near the end of baking
  10. Pull out your meal and serve
