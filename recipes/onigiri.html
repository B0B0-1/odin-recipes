Onigiri

Description

This is my take on onigiri with no filling. It's basic and a good place to start learning trying making these delicious rice balls. From this recipe, you can branch out into tons of different fillings and combination. These are fairly cheap and fast to make, and molding the rice can be really fun and satisfying.

This recipe is a mix of multiple I've tried over the last few years. I cobbled together the parts that worked for me, and now I have this recipe that my family loves. This recipe will only make a few onigiri, but you can easily scale it up for more. Give it a shot and see how you like them.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 1 jar of furikake seasoning
  • 1 pack of sushi nori
  • 1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
